Short Biography

I am currently a Research Associate Professor in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in September 2016, under the supervision of Professor Yuming Zhou. Before that, I received my Bachelor of Science degree from Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU) in June 2010.


Research

I'm interested in automated technologies for software and systems: testing and analysis.
